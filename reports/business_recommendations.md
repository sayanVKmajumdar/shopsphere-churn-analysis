## Executive Summary

ShopSphere has a **38% churn rate**, putting approximately
**$1.6 million in annual revenue at risk**. Three high-impact
actions can address the root causes immediately.

---

## Recommendation 1: Deploy an Instant Service-Recovery Program

**The Finding:**
Customers who filed a complaint in the last month have a churn
rate of 64% versus 27% for those who did not complain.
This is a 2.4x elevated risk and affects 30% of all customers.

**The Action:**
- When a complaint is filed, automatically send a personal
  email within 1 hour offering a 15% discount and free shipping
- For high-value accounts, follow up with a phone call within 24 hours
- Track whether the recovered customer purchases again within 30 days

**Expected Impact:**
Recovering just 30% of post-complaint churners saves approximately
450 customers per month, worth $38,000 in monthly revenue.

---

## Recommendation 2: Build a 6-Month Onboarding Retention Program

**The Finding:**
- Customers in months 0-3 churn at 67%
- Customers in months 3-6 churn at 58%
- After month 12, churn drops to under 20%

The first six months is where ShopSphere loses most customers.

**The Action:**
- Month 1: Welcome email series plus $20 cashback reward on second order
- Month 2: Personalized product recommendations via email
- Month 3: Loyalty milestone badge and early sale access
- Months 4-6: Monthly cashback bonus tied to order frequency
- Trigger: If a new customer goes 14 days without logging in,
  send a re-engagement notification with a 10% discount

**Expected Impact:**
Improving retention for new customers by 10 percentage points
saves approximately 175 customers per month.

---

## Recommendation 3: Fix the Delivery Experience for Distant Customers

**The Finding:**
- Customers under 10km from warehouse churn at 22%
- Customers over 30km from warehouse churn at 55%

Churn more than doubles as distance increases. These customers
also rate satisfaction 0.6 points lower on average, pointing
to delivery time and cost as the primary frustration.

**The Action:**
- Immediate: Offer free shipping for all customers beyond 25km
  (shipping subsidy of $6 is far cheaper than losing an $85/month customer)
- Short term: Audit the top 10 highest-churn zip codes for
  delivery delay patterns and switch carriers where needed
- Long term: Explore local pickup points or locker partnerships
  in high-distance, high-churn areas

**Expected Impact:**
Reducing churn for customers over 20km from 49% to 35%
saves approximately 200 customers per month.
